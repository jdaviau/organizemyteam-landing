Welcome to Team Manager! This guide will help you understand what Team Manager does and how it can simplify your role as a team treasurer.

## What is Team Manager?

Team Manager is a financial management platform built specifically for youth sports team treasurers. It helps you:

- **Track payments** from team members and families
- **Manage expenses** with categories and receipts
- **Generate reports** for board meetings and parent communication
- **Send reminders** automatically to families with outstanding balances
- **Stay organized** with a centralized dashboard for all your team's finances

## Who is Team Manager for?

Team Manager is designed for:

- **Team treasurers** managing dues, fees, and expenses
- **Team managers** who handle both logistics and finances
- **League administrators** overseeing multiple teams
- **Club officers** managing organization-wide finances

Whether you're managing a single youth soccer team or coordinating finances for an entire club, Team Manager scales to meet your needs.

## Key Features

### Payment Tracking

See exactly who owes what, when payments were made, and what's still outstanding. No more spreadsheet confusion or lost checks.

- Track multiple payment types (dues, tournament fees, equipment)
- Record cash, check, and online payments
- View payment history by family or by date
- Export data for your records

### Expense Management

Keep track of every dollar spent on behalf of the team:

- Categorize expenses (equipment, travel, facilities, etc.)
- Attach receipts for documentation
- Track reimbursements
- Compare spending to budget

### Automated Reminders

Stop the awkward "you still owe money" conversations:

- Set up automated reminder schedules
- Customize reminder messages
- Track who's received which reminders
- Let the system be the "bad guy"

### Financial Reports

Generate professional reports in seconds:

- Income and expense summaries
- Payment status by family
- Budget vs. actual comparisons
- End-of-season reports

## Getting Started

Ready to simplify your team's finances? Here's the quick path to getting started:

1. **Sign up** for a free account at [app.organizemyteam.com](https://app.organizemyteam.com/signup)
2. **Create your team** and add basic information
3. **Import or add** your roster of families
4. **Set up payment types** for your season
5. **Start tracking** payments as they come in

For a detailed walkthrough, see our [Quick Start Guide](/docs/getting-started/quick-start).
